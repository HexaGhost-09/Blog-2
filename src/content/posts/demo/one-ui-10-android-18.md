## How This List Was Built

Three rules drove every entry above:

1. **7-year flagships stay in**: any S-series phone still inside Samsung's promised major-OS window as of the projected ship date.
2. **Devices that hit their final update on One UI 9 drop off**: S23-series and anything similarly capped won't reappear.
3. **New flagships launch with it**: whatever ships in early-to-mid 2027 (S27, Fold 9/Flip 9, Tab S12) gets it out of the box, the same way S26 launched with One UI 8.5 and would carry One UI 9.

## Bottom Line

This is a forecast for planning purposes — useful if you're deciding whether to buy now or wait, but not something to quote as fact. Once Samsung actually opens a One UI 9.5 or One UI 10 beta program, the real list will look different in the details even if the broad shape holds. Check back closer to late 2026 for confirmed news.
